Looks like we will be seeing you at callbacks!

Please download the callback material for each role you are being called back for below. Each package may include scenes or script excerpts (“sides”), vocal music, and lyrics.

In theatre, “sides” are short sections of the script used during auditions and callbacks. Performers may be asked to read these scenes alone or with other actors in the room so we can explore character choices, chemistry, and different casting combinations together.

The biggest thing: do not stress. We are not looking for perfect.

Please do not worry about having everything fully memorised. You are absolutely welcome to print your material and bring it with you, and we will also have printed copies available at callbacks for performers to look over in the room.

We would much rather see you having fun, making strong acting choices, trying new things, and playing with the character than focusing only on memorising every single word.

Callbacks are meant to be collaborative, creative, and exciting. Come prepared, be open to direction, and have fun with the roles.
