What is this?
What things mean
Casting CardEach performer has a Casting Card showing their headshot, name, and age. This is what you move around when you're casting.
Sticky NoteEach Sticky Note is a character. Cards on a Sticky Note are people you're considering for that character.
Dark cardApplied — they asked to be considered for this character. No decision yet.
Normal cardLiked — you're seriously considering them for this character.
Glowing cardSelected — this is the person you've chosen for the character.
Symbols
△ ▢ ◯ ↔Shows what roles the performer is comfortable being cast in: △ girl/woman roles, ▢ boy/man roles, ◯ non-binary/neutral, ↔ any role.
C / A / S badgeWhat kind of part they want. C = chorus only, A = any role, S = specific roles only.
What you can do
Add to roleDrag a Casting Card onto a Sticky Note. They'll appear under that character.
MoveDrag a card from one Sticky Note to another.
RemoveDrag a card onto the empty board space. They stay in your Performers list.
SelectClick a card on a Sticky Note to toggle selected state (glow). Click again to deselect.
Character infoClick the character name on a Sticky Note to see role details.
UndoA toast appears after any change. Click Undo to reverse it immediately.
