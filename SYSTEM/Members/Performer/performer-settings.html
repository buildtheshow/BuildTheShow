Data Retention
How long we keep your information.

Your account information is retained while your account is active. Audition submission data is retained for 12 months after a production closes, then deleted or anonymized. If you delete your account, your personal information is permanently removed within 30 days. For questions, contact privacy@buildtheshow.com.
