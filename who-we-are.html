Who We Are

We are theatre people. The kind who have stood backstage with a headset on, trying to find someone who was supposed to be on five minutes ago.

We are the ones sending last-minute emails, updating spreadsheets at midnight, and wondering if anyone actually saw the message.

We have been the director, the volunteer wrangler, the person holding everything together with coffee and hope.

We know what it feels like when everything is almost working… and somehow still chaos.

We didn't mean to build an entire program… but here we are.

Why We Built This

Because nothing talks to each other.

Because auditions are in one place, volunteers in another, and half your communication is buried in group chats.

Because things get missed. People get confused. And you end up carrying way more than you should.

Because organizing a show should not feel like this.

So we built something that actually understands theatre.

One place. Everything connected.

All your production chaos, finally in one place.
